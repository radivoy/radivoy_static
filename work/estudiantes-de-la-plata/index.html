Estudiantes de La Plata

Estudiantes de La Plata is an Argentinian football club known as one of the most successful team in the country. It has won numerous league and international titles, including the Intercontinental Cup, becoming club world champions in 1968.

The goal of this project was to portray Estudiantes de La Plata according to the history of the club, and to provide news and information of the team in a responsive and modern website.

My role: UI / UX / Web development

In website's development I take part both in UI design and coding process, as in decisions for a better UX. The site was built using Wordpress CMS to benefit the client with updating site content.

Type & Colors

The decision was to follow the club’s traditional color palette and to find a very legible sans-serif typeface.
We created different styles to represent the overall visual aesthetic of the website and designed each element to create a seamless user experience.
